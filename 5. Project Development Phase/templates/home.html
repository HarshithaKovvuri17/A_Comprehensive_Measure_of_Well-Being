What is HDI

A Composite Measure of Human Development

The Human Development Index summarizes a country's average achievement in three dimensions: a long and healthy life, access to knowledge, and a decent standard of living. This model approximates HDI from observable indicators for each dimension, plus internet access as a modern connectivity proxy.

01 • Health

Life Expectancy

Years a newborn is expected to live given current mortality patterns.

02 • Education

Mean Years of Schooling

Average years of education received by people aged 25 and older.

03 • Income

GNI per Capita

Gross national income per person, adjusted for purchasing power.

04 • Connectivity

Internet Users

Share of the population using the internet, a modern development proxy.
